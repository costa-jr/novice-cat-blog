Are cats astronauts?

Cats are fascinating animals surrounded by mysteries.

With an independent and mysterious temperament, these felines are known for their agility, elegance, and often unpredictable behavior. However, recently, a theory has been gaining ground on the internet and among conspiracy theorists: could cats be astronauts?

The theory suggests that cats are not terrestrial animals, but rather extraterrestrial beings sent to carry out a specific mission on our planet.

There are historical reports that indicate cats were considered sacred beings in different cultures and even associated with divine figures. This could indicate that cats have a much more mystical origin than previously thought.

Moreover, there are theorists who believe that felines possess telepathic abilities and are capable of communicating with other alien species.

This belief is based on accounts from people who claim to have seen cats reacting to stimuli that are not perceivable to humans, or even behaving unusually during astronomical events, such as the aurora borealis.

These and other clues have been interpreted as signs that cats might be interplanetary beings.
